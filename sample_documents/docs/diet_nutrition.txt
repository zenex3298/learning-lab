Nutrition Guide for Optimal Performance

1. Macronutrient Balance:
   - Protein: Lean meats, fish, eggs, plant-based protein
   - Carbohydrates: Whole grains, fruits, vegetables
   - Fats: Healthy sources like nuts, avocados, olive oil

2. Meal Timing:
   - Pre-Workout: Carbohydrates for energy (e.g., banana, oatmeal)
   - Post-Workout: Protein and carbs for recovery (e.g., chicken & rice)
   - Hydration: Aim for at least 3L of water daily

3. Supplements (if needed):
   - Whey protein for muscle recovery
   - Omega-3 for joint health
   - Multivitamins for overall well-being
