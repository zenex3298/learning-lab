Sports Injury Recovery Guide

Common Injuries:
1. Sprains & Strains - Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation (RICE)
2. Tendonitis - Reduce activity, apply ice, and consider physical therapy.
3. Stress Fractures - Prioritize rest and avoid weight-bearing exercises.

Rehabilitation Tips:
- Low-impact activities like swimming or cycling aid recovery.
- Gradual return to training with focus on strength and mobility.
- Maintain a healthy diet to support healing (rich in protein and vitamins).

When to Seek Medical Attention:
- Severe pain or swelling that persists.
- Inability to bear weight or move the affected area.
- Signs of infection (redness, fever, pus).
