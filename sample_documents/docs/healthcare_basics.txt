Healthcare Basics: Understanding Preventative Care

1. Annual Check-Ups:
   - Visit your doctor for routine blood tests, screenings, and vaccinations.
   - Early detection of conditions like diabetes or high blood pressure is key.

2. Immunization Schedule:
   - Flu shot (annual), Tetanus booster (every 10 years), COVID-19 boosters as recommended.

3. Healthy Lifestyle Choices:
   - Balanced diet, regular exercise, and sufficient sleep contribute to long-term health.
   - Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
   - Manage stress through relaxation techniques.
