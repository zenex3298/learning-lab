Mental Health & Well-Being Strategies

1. Daily Mindfulness:
   - Practice 5-10 minutes of meditation or deep breathing.
   - Journaling to track thoughts and emotions.

2. Stress Management:
   - Engage in hobbies (reading, music, painting).
   - Limit screen time and ensure quality sleep.

3. Building Resilience:
   - Set achievable goals and track small wins.
   - Develop positive self-talk habits.
   - Connect with friends, family, or a therapist when needed.
